What is NBA Basketball School?

NBA Basketball School is a network of tuition-based basketball development programs open to male and female players from outside the U.S. ages 6-18. NBA Basketball Schools have been launched in Brazil, China, Greece, India, Mexico, Uruguay and the UAE. The NBA Basketball School curriculum is designed to help players, parents, coaches and organizations better understand the process of improvement and development. The program focuses on oncourt training, strength and conditioning, and basketball education under the direction of local coaches who work directly with the NBA International Basketball Operations staff around the world.
